On Pyramid Schemes

Here at Triangle, we sell triangles, so of course we hate pyramids.

However, some people seem to confuse Triangle with a pyramid scheme.

To start, pyramid schemes recruit people. At Triangle, we do not recruit people, as everyone is already part of the team! We simply convince more people to be part of the salesforce. Thus, Triangle cannot be a pyramid scheme.

Even if you don't believe that, the person that recruited convinced me to start selling told me that Triangle is more like a multi-level marketing company. This is because in a pyramid scheme, there is "no real product being sold" (source). However, as mentioned many times before, here at Triangle, we sell triangles. We even have a shop page!
