How it works

Here at Triangle, we sell triangles of all kind - equilateral, right, large, imaginary, you name it.

However, selling triangles isn't easy, and we need YOUR help.

Here's how it works: initially, you will not make any money from selling triangles, but your work will not go un-noticed by the company. You will be compensated with non-monetary rewards such as a reduction in work hours from 12 hours per day to 11.

You will start to make money once you recruit people. For every person you personally recruit, you will make 20 cents more per triangle that you sell.
